GovTech Creators

Session: GovTech Creators

Target Audience: Government Teachers

GovTech Creators was a transformative session designed for educators serving in public sector institutions. Focused on media literacy and video production, it trained teachers on scripting, storyboarding, filming, editing, and publishing classroom videos that are impactful and engaging. Participants included senior educators and officials from the education department, making it a high-impact initiative for grassroots digital transformation.

  • Objective: Build capacity among teachers in digital storytelling and classroom media production.
  • Tools Taught: Smartphone filming, free editing apps, framing techniques, lighting tips.
  • Pedagogy: Hands-on activities, live recording simulations, and real-world classroom scenarios.
  • Outcome: Enabled teachers to independently create educational content using minimal resources.
